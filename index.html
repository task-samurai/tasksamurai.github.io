get in touch

Are you tired or searching through thousands of code files just to fix that one-line bug?

How about helping out your coworkers on that weird code from the component you never worked on?

Task Samurai helps you focus on the fun part of coding! It learns from your history of code changes and tickets so that, when you pick up that new fresh ticket, you get instant recommendations of which files you likely need to change to fulfill the ticket!


Stop spending your time going through thousands of code files to find that small chunk of code you need to change. Instead, focus your time writing the meaningful code that only you can write!

Step 1: connect your favorite tools

Connect your source-code provider and ticket management system. Optionally, connect your IDE to get recommendations directly into your workspace!

Step 2: let the magic happen

Task Samurai will take up to a couple of hours to sync your project, learning from your history of code changes and previous tickets.

Step 3: get code recommendations

Get your recommendations either in tickets you are working on or directly in your favorite IDE! No need to change how you work, Task Samurai adapts so you don't have to!
